## The project at a glance

### Phase 1 — The Rebuild (8 weeks · $20,000)
- New Wix site replacing WordPress/Elementor/Playbook
- 6 membership SKUs (Intermediate + Advanced × 1-day/2-day/Unlimited)
- Member self-service portal (upgrade, cancel, book)
- Square integration for shop + in-person
- CourtReserve for attendance entitlement enforcement
- Meta Business Suite untangling + social embeds
- SEO foundation · accessibility · domain cutover

### Phase 2 — The Growth Layer ($2,500/mo retainer)
- Live match score tracking (ATP · USTA · NCAA · Big NC local)
- Coach scheduling + calendarization
- Tennis blog with live feed of recent results + upcoming tournaments + brackets
- Local tournament builder with auto-generated brackets
- AI coaching program (SmartSwing + SwingVision + Claude)
- Content engine per the Filveo-style GTM bible
- Lightweight CRM + per-persona email cadences

### Phase 3 — The Moat
- Big NC Foundation (Title I outreach)
- Big NC Coach Academy (PTR-partnered certification)
- Corporate wellness + HOA court management
- The Big NC Method™ trademark
- Franchise-ready playbook
