The Situation

Big NC is ready to level up. The platform isn't.

Big NC Tennis already has the hardest thing to build: five locations, real coaching reputation, named personalities parents trust, and multi-program depth. What it lacks is the digital foundation to monetize those assets. That's what this project delivers.

Before

Slow WordPress.
No pricing.
No portal.

Members manage subscriptions through your inbox. Prices hide behind a contact form. One-day members show up four days. SEO is empty. Social is tangled with an old personal account.

Phase 1 · 8 weeks

Rebuild the platform.

New Wix site. 6 membership tiers. Member self-service portal. Square + Wix Payments. CourtReserve entitlement enforcement. Social untangled. Shop live. SEO foundation.

Phase 2+ · Ongoing

Compound the moat.

AI coaching program. Content engine per GTM bible. Live scores + local brackets. Corporate + HOA prospecting. Charlotte expansion. A defensible brand that no NC competitor can copy in under 12 months.

Tab 5 · Strategy Snapshot

Where Big NC wins. Where it doesn't. What it builds.

The full strategic study lives in Big-NC-Tennis-Strategy.md. This is the one-page version.

SWOT — at a glance

Strengths
  • 5 locations (rare for non-municipal NC operator)
  • Named coach personalities · parent endorsements
  • Pickleball + tennis diversification
  • Prototype design system already built
  • Founder-led decision speed
Weaknesses
  • WordPress drag · no SEO surface
  • Pricing hidden · leaks leads to competitors
  • No member self-service
  • Name recognition below Cary Tennis Park
  • Attendance policing is manual
Opportunities
  • AI coaching stack — unique in NC
  • Charlotte Sugaw Creek · blank market
  • HOA court management contracts
  • Corporate wellness (Duke, SAS, Lenovo, BoA)
  • Title I outreach · USTA Southern grants
  • Pickleball crossover narrative
Threats
  • Cary Tennis Park municipal pricing
  • Country clubs upgrading tennis (River Run, RRC)
  • Drop-in price wars (Charlotte Tennis Academy $35)
  • Instructor talent war
  • Facility lease dependency

The positioning

The strategic bet

Be the club that treats every member like a pro — with AI-driven weekly plans, coach accountability, and neighborhood convenience — for $119–$399/mo instead of $75,000/yr.
